Lasagna

Description

Good for people who aren't reluctant to try tofu. After this lasagna you and your guests will love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna noodles (16 oz)
  • 6 cups meat sauce
  • 16 oz. ricotta
  • 16 oz. shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Prepare lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375. Spray two 9x13 pans with non-stick cooking spray.
  3. Mix 3 cups of meat sauce with the ricotta cheese. Season to taste.
  4. Place 1/2 cup of sauce in 9x13 dish.
  5. Lay 3 lasagna noodles side-by-side in the dish. Spread some of the ricotta mixture over the noodles and top with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat and top the 4th layer with cheese.
  7. Repeat the procedure for the second lasagna.
  8. At this point, you can tightly wrap the dishes with foil and freeze or bake.
  9. Bake uncovered for 30 minures.
  10. If frozen, bake covered for 45 minutes and uncovered for 15 minutes.
