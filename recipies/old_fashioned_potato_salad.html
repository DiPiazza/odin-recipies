Old Fashioned Potato Salad

Description

There are many ways to make potato salad, but if you are looking for a traditional, mayonnaise-based potato salad made with hard-boiled eggs, celery, and a little pickle relish for zing, you've landed on the right recipie. With more than one thousand ratings and newarly a thousand reviews from our Allrecipes community of home cooks, this recipe for Old-Fashioned Potato salad is proven winner. And it's so easy to make using everyday ingredients. I'll show you how to make Ol-Fashioned Potato salad, with loads of tips all along the way. Plus, I'll share tips for storing and freezing potato salad. (Yes, you can freeze potato salad!)

Ingridients

  • 5 Potatoes
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 Cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 Cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 Teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon celery salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, peel and chop.
  2. While potatoes cook, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for about 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.
  3. Combine the potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt, celery salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix together well and refrigerate until chilled.
